about

Meet Kamikaze Legacy, a young, self-taught artist with a love for rich and full compositions. Based in Poland, the composer began learning at the age of 13 with electronic music, but dabbles in a wide array of genres today, primarily with orchestral scores. His work can be found not only on his own page, but within numerous different video game and short film projects, creating both music and sound effects with his unique sound design and recognizable style. The composer prides himself on telling an original and fascinating story in each of his songs, pouring bits of himself into everything he creates.

bio

Kamikaze Legacy, a 21-year-old university student majoring in sound engineering, has 8 years of experience in music-making. During those 8 years, he tinkered with music to not just learn what goes into it, but to make that craft his own. He uses not just the most widely understood instruments, but also the fresh sounds of the often unheard instruments from different cultures. Both sounds, new and old, can be found within his pieces. He prides himself on using them in a new distinct way, entirely unique to his projects and style. His sound is used to exemplify his rich storytelling within his music. The composer is versatile, working well within many different genres and styles, and enjoying the challenge that comes along with different sounds.
